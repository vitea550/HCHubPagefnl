HC#9: Flyers!

~ Alex Vitek

As attention becomes an increasingly hot commodity in an era inundated with information, grassroots ways of getting messages to people remain important to cut through an ever-so-saturated digital market. Not leaving things up to chance or an algorithim, flyers and physical advertising are still important for getting artists and events on people's radars. I personally have numerous experiences with images, art, and people that I see exclusively in flyers and on the street.

An exercise in good design, flyers need to have clear and legible hierarchy, especially from distance. Often making use of text sizing, color, and how such elements can help guide the eye, flyers are interesting not only because of their organic approach to advertising, but method and construction for being noticed.
